PRIVACY POLICY HERO’S PRIVACY POLICY

HERO’s mission is to provide reliable skilled home services in an open-market environment through a mobile platform to empower the Filipino household. As such, HERO acknowledge and recognize its responsibilities under Republic Act No. 10173 also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012, with respect to the data collected, recorded, organized, updated, used, consolidated or destroyed, in relation to the platform and/or HERO app.

This policy outlines how HERO collect, use, process and disclose your Personal Data through the use of the HERO app and/or platform. This shall apply to the users, both Service Providers and Service Users (collectively referred to as “you”, “your” or “yours”). From time to time, we may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the procedure and the law. Rest assured that we will notify you by posting it on the platform and/or HERO app for your information and reference.

As Service User, you signify that you have read, understood and consented to the collection and use of your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Likewise, by consenting to the Agreement outlining the terms and conditions of use of the platform and/or HERO app, upon availment and use of any of the services, it shall be understood that you have agreed to the collection and processing of your data.

Further, we acknowledge that you have the right to be informed whether your personal information were, are, or will be processed. HERO is more than happy to provide you with the general facts on how your personal information are collected, handled and processed so long as the request is not arbitrary and vexatious and shall not pertain to confidential or proprietary information.

WHAT is collected?
“Personal Data” is any information, whether recorded in a material form or not, from which the identity of an individual is apparent or can be reasonably and directly ascertained by the entity holding the information, or when put together with other information would directly and certainly identify an individual.

“Sensitive personal information” refers to personal information:
1. About an individual’s race, ethnic origin, marital status, age, color, and religious, philosophical or political affiliations;

2. About an individual’s health, education, genetic or sexual life of a person, or to any proceeding for any offense committed or alleged to have been committed by such individual, the disposal of such proceedings, or the sentence of any court in such proceedings;

3. Issued by government agencies peculiar to an individual which includes but is not limited to, social security numbers, previous or current health records, licenses or its denials, suspension or revocation, and tax returns; and

4. Specifically established by an executive order or an act of Congress to be kept classified.

Full access to our services made available through the platform and/or HERO app is only possible after creation and registration of an account where you would be required to provide the following information: your name, date of birth, address, sex, mobile number, e-mail address and a password.

You also share data with us by sending queries through the platform and/or HERO app, submitting a service request, reporting customer service issues, and by taking part in our research and surveys.

When you open the HERO app, we may automatically record information such as your IP address, device location, pages or links you access on the platform and/or HERO app, the time spent on those pages and links, items and information searched for on the platform and/or HERO app, date and time of access, and other statistics. We may also use cookies, web beacons, small data text files, or similar technologies to identify your device.

We assure you that the processing of personal data and other information collected is based on a legitimate business interest and shall in no way supersede your fundamental rights or freedoms.
